The Deep-Time Global Change Group at Peking University officially launched [LabHub](https://lab.mingsongli.com/) on April 25, 2026. LabHub is a research management platform developed and managed by OpenClaw for the group, designed to help connect everyday supervision, research progress, member development, and group communication.

Core functions include research progress tracking, where each research task, or Quest, is organized into 9 stages and 27 checkpoints; member profiles covering research directions, skills, papers, conferences, and training milestones for PhD students, postdoctoral researchers, and undergraduate students; one-on-one meeting records for tracking discussions and action items; group meeting scheduling; integrated email for communication and notification delivery; and an EXP and level system that turns research progress into visible milestones.

The system supports several common group-management needs: supervisors can follow each member's research progress in real time, members can check their own milestones and next steps, and the group can visualize annual research development more clearly.
